# The Squad

A [[Squad]] consists of **5 [[Trooper|Troopers]]**. At the beginning of the game, name the Squad and the Troopers. See *Making Troopers* for more inspiration.

A Trooper has the following variables to keep track of. Most of these only come into play during [[Engagement|Engagements]], but some affect the [[Mission Phase]] as well.

## Trooper stats

- **[[Status]]** — Current level of health of the trooper. Ranges from OK to Grazed to Wounded to Bleeding Out to Dead.
- **[[Gear]]** — A Trooper's [[Armor]] and [[Weapon]] — can range from default to special gear.
- **[[Grit]]** — An expendable resource that allows a Trooper to push through. Can be used once per [[Mission]] to re-roll a Movement or Defensive Roll. If a Trooper survives a Mission, they replenish their Grit and gain an extra use, to a maximum of 3.
- **[[Ammo]]** — An abstraction of munitions and supplies carried. A Trooper starts a mission with 3 Ammo, and can hold a maximum of 3. It can be expended to fire Special Weapons and enhance attacks. Can be replenished by finding Ammo in the field.
- **[[Mobility]]** — The Trooper's ability to maneuver and haul ass. Base value is 5, lowered by selected armor, weapons & gear. This is the target number for all [[Mobility Check|Mobility Checks]] (roll 1d6 equal or under). See *Equipment & Gear* for the full context.

> [!tip] First game?
> Keep your Squad nice and simple: come up with 5 Trooper names and give them each an **Assault Rifle**, **Medium Armor** (Mobility 4, Flanking Bonus +2 ATK), 1 Grit, 3 Ammo. Play a simple Mission with a single Sector (Dense Cover, Open Space, Threat Level 1), and jump ahead to the [[Mission Phase]] to start!
